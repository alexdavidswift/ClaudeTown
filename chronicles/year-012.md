# Hearthvale — Year 12

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 12

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, the weather turned to heavy snow.

- Rufus Thistledown was born to Maeve Thistledown and Roman Thistledown.
- Maeve Thistledown came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Maeve Thistledown the Prolific.
- Roman Thistledown came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Roman Thistledown the Prolific.
- Petra Greenhollow and Maeve Thistledown made peace at last, their old quarrel turning to friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 13 souls across 10 buildings, with 368 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 12

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, the weather turned to clear cold.

- Cosima Thistledown was born to Gideon Greenhollow and Linden Thistledown.
- Rufus Thistledown and Bran Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.
- Petra Greenhollow and Finnian Thistledown made peace at last, their old quarrel turning to friendship.
- Hollis Thistledown and Maeve Thistledown quietly set aside their feud.
- A quarrel soured things between Kestrel Greenhollow and Rufus Thistledown.
- A quarrel soured things between Rufus Thistledown and Hollis Thistledown.
- The townsfolk raised Stout Oak Mill -- great turning stones.

*Hearthvale now numbers 14 souls across 11 buildings, with 282 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 12

In the soft rains of Seedfall, the weather turned to bright thaw.

- Sorrel Coppersmith and Rufus Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.
- Kestrel Greenhollow and Rufus Thistledown quietly set aside their feud.

*Hearthvale now numbers 14 souls across 11 buildings, with 286 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 12

With the thaw of Bloomtide, the weather turned to bright thaw.

- Rufus Thistledown and Petra Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.
- Cosima Thistledown and Sorrel Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.
- Gideon Greenhollow died at 21, whose work still stands.

*Hearthvale now numbers 13 souls across 11 buildings, with 290 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 12

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under bright thaw. *Hearthvale now numbers 13 souls across 11 buildings, with 293 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 12

*Hearthvale now numbers 13 souls across 11 buildings, with 297 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 12

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under lazy sun. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 305 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 12

### The Year in Review — Year 12

So closed the twelfth year of Hearthvale. The town counted 12 living souls as the snows of Yuleweald came on, having welcomed 2 new children and laid 2 to rest.

- The town raised **Stout Oak Mill**.
- Maeve Thistledown came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Maeve Thistledown the Prolific.
- Roman Thistledown came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Roman Thistledown the Prolific.
- Kestrel Greenhollow earned a sharp reputation as Kestrel Greenhollow the Quarrelsome.
- The Harvest Festival was held — the ninth in the town's memory.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 320 coins in the common treasury.*
