# Hearthvale — Year 1

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Thawmoon, Year 1

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, the weather turned to grey sleet.

- A quarrel soured things between Maeve Thistledown and Petra Greenhollow.
- The townsfolk raised Low Hollow Well -- the town's source of fresh water.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 6 buildings, with 25 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 1

In the soft rains of Seedfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under bright thaw. *Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 6 buildings, with 27 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 1

With the thaw of Bloomtide, the weather turned to bright thaw.

- Petra Greenhollow and Gideon Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.
- A quarrel soured things between Cosima Underhill and Linnea Coppersmith.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 6 buildings, with 29 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 1
