# Hearthvale — Year 4

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 4

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under bitter frost. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 28 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 4

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, the weather turned to clear cold.

- A quarrel soured things between Gideon Greenhollow and Briony Coppersmith.
- A fever passed through Hearthvale, laying 3 of the townsfolk low.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 33 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 4

In the soft rains of Seedfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under bright thaw. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 38 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 4

With the thaw of Bloomtide, the weather turned to green haze.

- Gideon Greenhollow and Roman Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 43 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 4

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, the weather turned to bright thaw.

- A quarrel soured things between Linden Thistledown and Linnea Coppersmith.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 48 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 4

In the golden light of Hayrest, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under thunderheads. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 53 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 4

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under golden heat. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 58 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 4

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under long dusk. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 63 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 4

Under amber skies in Mistgather, the weather turned to falling leaves.

- A quarrel soured things between Roman Thistledown and Cosima Underhill.
- The Harvest Festival filled the square with lanterns, music, and the smell of spiced cider.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 68 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 4

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, the weather turned to crisp wind.

- Maeve Thistledown and Briony Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 73 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 4

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under amber light. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 79 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 4

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, the weather turned to bitter frost.

- Petra Greenhollow and Cosima Underhill struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 84 coins in the common treasury.*
