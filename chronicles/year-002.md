# Hearthvale — Year 2

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 2

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under heavy snow. *Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 6 buildings, with 52 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 2

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, the weather turned to clear cold.

- Linden Thistledown and Petra Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 6 buildings, with 54 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 2

In the soft rains of Seedfall, the weather turned to first warmth.

- A quarrel soured things between Maeve Thistledown and Linden Thistledown.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 6 buildings, with 56 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 2

With the thaw of Bloomtide, the weather turned to blustery showers.

- Cosima Underhill and Bran Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.
- A quarrel soured things between Gideon Greenhollow and Cosima Underhill.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 6 buildings, with 58 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 2

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, the weather turned to blustery showers.

- The townsfolk raised Whispering Hollow Market -- stalls of trade and gossip.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 7 buildings, with 0 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 2

In the golden light of Hayrest, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under dry wind. *Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 7 buildings, with 3 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 2

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, the weather turned to golden heat.

- Gideon Greenhollow and Linnea Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 7 buildings, with 8 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 2

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under lazy sun. *Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 7 buildings, with 13 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 2

Under amber skies in Mistgather, the weather turned to early mist.

- Briony Coppersmith was born to Linnea Coppersmith and Bran Coppersmith.
- The Harvest Festival filled the square with lanterns, music, and the smell of spiced cider.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 18 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 2

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, the weather turned to amber light.

- A fever passed through Hearthvale, laying 2 of the townsfolk low.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 23 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 2

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under harvest sun. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 28 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 2

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, the weather turned to still chill.

- Linden Thistledown and Gideon Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.
- Briony Coppersmith and Linnea Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.
- A quarrel soured things between Bran Coppersmith and Linnea Coppersmith.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 33 coins in the common treasury.*
