# Hearthvale — Year 5

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 5

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, the weather turned to still chill.

- Edmund Thistledown was born to Maeve Thistledown and Roman Thistledown.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 8 buildings, with 89 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 5

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, the weather turned to heavy snow.

- Sorrel Coppersmith was born to Linnea Coppersmith and Bran Coppersmith.
- The townsfolk raised Old Raven Tavern -- warmth, ale, and song.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 9 buildings, with 14 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 5

In the soft rains of Seedfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under soft rain. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 9 buildings, with 19 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 5

With the thaw of Bloomtide, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under first warmth. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 9 buildings, with 24 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 5

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, the weather turned to green haze.

- Sorrel Coppersmith and Maeve Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 9 buildings, with 29 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 5

In the golden light of Hayrest, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under dry wind. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 9 buildings, with 35 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 5

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, the weather turned to lazy sun.

- Briony Coppersmith died at 2, whose stories outlived the telling.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 41 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 5

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under golden heat. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 46 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 5

Under amber skies in Mistgather, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under crisp wind. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 52 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 5

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under falling leaves. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 58 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 5

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, the weather turned to amber light.

- Maeve Thistledown and Edmund Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 64 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 5

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under grey sleet. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 69 coins in the common treasury.*
