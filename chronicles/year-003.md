# Hearthvale — Year 3

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 3

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, the weather turned to heavy snow.

- Petra Greenhollow and Briony Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 38 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 3

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, the weather turned to bitter frost.

- Linnea Coppersmith and Petra Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.
- A quarrel soured things between Briony Coppersmith and Roman Thistledown.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 43 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 3

In the soft rains of Seedfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under green haze. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 48 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 3

With the thaw of Bloomtide, the weather turned to green haze.

- A quarrel soured things between Briony Coppersmith and Linden Thistledown.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 53 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 3

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under green haze. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 58 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 3

In the golden light of Hayrest, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under golden heat. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 63 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 3

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, the weather turned to dry wind.

- Roman Thistledown and Petra Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 7 buildings, with 68 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 3

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, the weather turned to long dusk.

- A quarrel soured things between Linden Thistledown and Cosima Underhill.
- The townsfolk raised Stout Raven Smithy -- the ring of hammer on anvil.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 3 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 3

Under amber skies in Mistgather, the weather turned to early mist.

- The Harvest Festival filled the square with lanterns, music, and the smell of spiced cider.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 8 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 3

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under crisp wind. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 13 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 3

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, the weather turned to early mist.

- A quarrel soured things between Viktor Greenhollow and Briony Coppersmith.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 18 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 3

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under still chill. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 8 buildings, with 23 coins in the common treasury.*
