# Hearthvale — Year 7

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 7

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, the weather turned to heavy snow.

- A quarrel soured things between Maeve Thistledown and Cosima Underhill.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 138 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 7

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under bitter frost. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 143 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 7

In the soft rains of Seedfall, the weather turned to bright thaw.

- Cosima Underhill and Linnea Coppersmith quietly set aside their feud.
- Linnea Coppersmith died at 41, whose work still stands.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 9 buildings, with 148 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 7

With the thaw of Bloomtide, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under first warmth. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 9 buildings, with 152 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 7

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, the weather turned to green haze.

- Bran Coppersmith and Cosima Underhill were wed beneath the spring sky.
- Petra Greenhollow and Maeve Thistledown quietly set aside their feud.
- A fever passed through Hearthvale, laying 2 of the townsfolk low.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 9 buildings, with 156 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 7

In the golden light of Hayrest, the weather turned to thunderheads.

- Petra Greenhollow and Sorrel Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.
- Gideon Greenhollow and Maeve Thistledown quietly set aside their feud.
- Cosima Underhill died at 47, gone to the quiet fields.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 9 buildings, with 160 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 7

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under thunderheads. *Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 9 buildings, with 163 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 7

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, the weather turned to long dusk.

- Bran Coppersmith and Maeve Thistledown made peace at last, their old quarrel turning to friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 9 buildings, with 167 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 7

Under amber skies in Mistgather, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under falling leaves. *Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 9 buildings, with 171 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 7

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under harvest sun. *Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 9 buildings, with 175 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 7

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under harvest sun. *Hearthvale now numbers 9 souls across 9 buildings, with 179 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 7

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, the weather turned to heavy snow.

- Hollis Thistledown was born to Maeve Thistledown and Roman Thistledown.
- Viktor Greenhollow and Edmund Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.
- Edmund Thistledown and Sorrel Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.
- A fever passed through Hearthvale, laying 5 of the townsfolk low.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 9 buildings, with 182 coins in the common treasury.*


---

### The Year in Review — Year 7

So closed the seventh year of Hearthvale. The town counted 10 living souls as the snows of Yuleweald came on, having welcomed 1 new child and laid 2 to rest.

- **1 wedding** were celebrated.
- The town endured hardship: A fever passed through Hearthvale, laying 2 of the townsfolk low — the second such trial this year, and the fourth in all its years.

*Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 9 buildings, with 182 coins in the common treasury.*
