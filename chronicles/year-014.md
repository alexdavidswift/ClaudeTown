# Hearthvale — Year 14

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 14

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under clear cold. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 364 coins in the common treasury.*

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 367 coins in the common treasury.*
