# Hearthvale — Year 8

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under heavy snow. *Hearthvale now numbers 10 souls across 9 buildings, with 185 coins in the common treasury.*
