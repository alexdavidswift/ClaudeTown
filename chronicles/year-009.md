# Hearthvale — Year 9

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 9

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, the weather turned to bitter frost.

- Edmund Thistledown and Finnian Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 229 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 9

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under still chill. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 233 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 9

In the soft rains of Seedfall, the weather turned to green haze.

- A fire gutted Cottage; neighbors took the family in while it was rebuilt.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 237 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 9

With the thaw of Bloomtide, the weather turned to bright thaw.

- A quarrel soured things between Hollis Thistledown and Maeve Thistledown.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 241 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 9

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under green haze. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 245 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 9

In the golden light of Hayrest, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under dry wind. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 250 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 9

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under long dusk. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 255 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 9

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under lazy sun. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 260 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 9

Under amber skies in Mistgather, the weather turned to amber light.

- The Harvest Festival filled the square with lanterns, music, and the smell of spiced cider.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 265 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 9

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under early mist. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 270 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 9

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, the weather turned to early mist.

- Finnian Thistledown and Sorrel Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 275 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 9

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, the weather turned to grey sleet.

- Bran Coppersmith and Gideon Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.
- A quarrel soured things between Finnian Thistledown and Petra Greenhollow.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 279 coins in the common treasury.*


---

### The Year in Review — Year 9

So closed the ninth year of Hearthvale. The town counted 11 living souls as the snows of Yuleweald came on.

- The Harvest Festival was held — the sixth in the town's memory.
- The town endured hardship: A fire gutted Cottage; neighbors took the family in while it was rebuilt — the first such trial this year, and the fifth in all its years.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 279 coins in the common treasury.*
