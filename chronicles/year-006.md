# Hearthvale — Year 6

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 6

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under still chill. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 74 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 6

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, the weather turned to grey sleet.

- An old well, long dry, suddenly ran sweet and cold again.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 79 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 6

In the soft rains of Seedfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under bright thaw. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 84 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 6

With the thaw of Bloomtide, the weather turned to first warmth.

- Roman Thistledown and Linden Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 89 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 6

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, the weather turned to soft rain.

- Roman Thistledown and Viktor Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.
- Bran Coppersmith and Sorrel Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 94 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 6

In the golden light of Hayrest, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under thunderheads. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 99 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 6

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under dry wind. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 105 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 6

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under thunderheads. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 110 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 6

Under amber skies in Mistgather, the weather turned to crisp wind.

- Viktor Greenhollow and Sorrel Coppersmith struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 116 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 6

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, the weather turned to falling leaves.

- A quarrel soured things between Edmund Thistledown and Linnea Coppersmith.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 122 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 6

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under crisp wind. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 128 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 6

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under heavy snow. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 133 coins in the common treasury.*
