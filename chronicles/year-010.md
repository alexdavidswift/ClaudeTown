# Hearthvale — Year 10

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 10

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under still chill. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 283 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 10

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, the weather turned to grey sleet.

- Finnian Thistledown and Linden Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 287 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 10

In the soft rains of Seedfall, the weather turned to soft rain.

- Bran Coppersmith and Viktor Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.
- Gideon Greenhollow and Maeve Thistledown made peace at last, their old quarrel turning to friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 291 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 10

With the thaw of Bloomtide, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under soft rain. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 295 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 10

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, the weather turned to soft rain.

- Edmund Thistledown and Roman Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 299 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 10

In the golden light of Hayrest, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under long dusk. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 304 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 10

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, the weather turned to golden heat.

- Viktor Greenhollow came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Viktor Greenhollow the Wealthy.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 308 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 10

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, the weather turned to dry wind.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 313 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 10

Under amber skies in Mistgather, the weather turned to amber light.

- Hollis Thistledown and Maeve Thistledown quietly set aside their feud.
- The Harvest Festival filled the square with lanterns, music, and the smell of spiced cider.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 318 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 10

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, the weather turned to harvest sun.

- Gideon Greenhollow and Viktor Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 323 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 10

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under early mist. *Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 328 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 10

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, the weather turned to clear cold.

- A quarrel soured things between Hollis Thistledown and Maeve Thistledown.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 332 coins in the common treasury.*

### The Year in Review — Year 10

So closed the tenth year of Hearthvale. The town counted 11 living souls as the snows of Yuleweald came on.

- Viktor Greenhollow came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Viktor Greenhollow the Wealthy.
- Petra Greenhollow came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Petra Greenhollow the Wealthy.
- The Harvest Festival was held — the seventh in the town's memory.

*Hearthvale now numbers 11 souls across 9 buildings, with 332 coins in the common treasury.*
