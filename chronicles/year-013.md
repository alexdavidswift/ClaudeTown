# Hearthvale — Year 13

_From the Chronicle of Hearthvale, founded Frostwane, Year 1._

### Frostwane, Year 13

Snow lay on the rooftops as Frostwane, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under still chill. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 323 coins in the common treasury.*

### Thawmoon, Year 13

Through the short cold days of Thawmoon, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under heavy snow. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 326 coins in the common treasury.*

### Seedfall, Year 13

In the soft rains of Seedfall, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under green haze. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 329 coins in the common treasury.*

### Bloomtide, Year 13

With the thaw of Bloomtide, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under soft rain. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 332 coins in the common treasury.*

### Sunhigh, Year 13

As the first green returned in Sunhigh, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under bright thaw. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 335 coins in the common treasury.*

### Hayrest, Year 13

In the golden light of Hayrest, the weather turned to dry wind.

- Bran Coppersmith came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Bran Coppersmith the Wealthy.
- Kestrel Greenhollow and Cosima Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.
- Linden Thistledown and Kestrel Greenhollow quietly set aside their feud.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 339 coins in the common treasury.*

### Goldmaize, Year 13

Under the long sun of Goldmaize, the weather turned to thunderheads.

- Sorrel Coppersmith and Roman Thistledown struck up a fast friendship.
- Finnian Thistledown and Kestrel Greenhollow made peace at last, their old quarrel turning to friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 342 coins in the common treasury.*

### Emberfall, Year 13

Through the warm weeks of Emberfall, the weather turned to long dusk.

- Finnian Thistledown and Viktor Greenhollow quietly set aside their feud.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 346 coins in the common treasury.*

### Mistgather, Year 13

Under amber skies in Mistgather, the weather turned to harvest sun.

- A quarrel soured things between Hollis Thistledown and Finnian Thistledown.
- The Harvest Festival filled the square with lanterns, music, and the smell of spiced cider.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 350 coins in the common treasury.*

### Leafdown, Year 13

As the leaves turned in Leafdown, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under amber light. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 354 coins in the common treasury.*

### Snowcome, Year 13

Through the harvest weeks of Snowcome, Hearthvale passed a quiet month under amber light. *Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 358 coins in the common treasury.*

### Yuleweald, Year 13

Under a hard frost in Yuleweald, the weather turned to bitter frost.

- Hollis Thistledown and Viktor Greenhollow struck up a fast friendship.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 361 coins in the common treasury.*


---

### The Year in Review — Year 13

So closed the thirteenth year of Hearthvale. The town counted 12 living souls as the snows of Yuleweald came on.

- Bran Coppersmith came to be known throughout Hearthvale as Bran Coppersmith the Wealthy.
- The Harvest Festival was held — the tenth in the town's memory.

*Hearthvale now numbers 12 souls across 11 buildings, with 361 coins in the common treasury.*
